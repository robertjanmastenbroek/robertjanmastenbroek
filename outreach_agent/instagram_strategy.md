## CONVERSION TACTICS

### Instagram Music Sticker — Track Rotation Schedule
Use Instagram's native Spotify music sticker in Stories. Rotate these tracks:
- Week 1: Living Water (122 BPM — most accessible entry point)
- Week 2: Renamed (130 BPM — tribal, strong hook)
- Week 3: Halleluyah (140 BPM — if audience is warming to heavier)
- Week 4: He Is The Light (122 BPM — faith-forward audience)
- Week 5: Fire In Our Hands (130 BPM — high energy)
- Repeat cycle, inserting new releases immediately when they drop

### "Add to Spotify" Feature
When posting Reel or Feed post with a specific track:
1. Tag the Spotify track using Instagram's music tag feature
2. This adds "Play on Spotify" button directly to your post
3. Use the caption CTA: "Tap the music tag above to save it to Spotify"
4. Combine with text: "Save it — it tells Spotify to recommend it to others"

### Collaboration Tags for Cross-Audience Reach
Tag these types of accounts when relevant — they have overlapping audiences:
- Fellow tribal/psytrance producers (they often re-share mentions)
- Tenerife-based accounts (@visitTenerife, local venue accounts)
- Faith + music crossover accounts (Christian music blogs, faith-based creators)
- Festival accounts where the genre fits (Ozora, BoomFestival, Hadra)
- Organic house / melodic techno accounts (larger audience, accessible entry)

**Rule:** Only tag when genuinely relevant — forced tags hurt reach.

### Pinned Posts Strategy (maintain 3 pinned posts at all times)
- **Pin 1:** The story post — €6M, rebuild, Tenerife (highest-converting for new followers)
- **Pin 2:** Most recent track release post (always updated on new release)
- **Pin 3:** Best Friday Sunset Session reel (shows live event, local identity)
