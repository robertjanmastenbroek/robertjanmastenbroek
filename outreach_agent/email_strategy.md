# Fan Email List Strategy — Robert-Jan Mastenbroek

*Strategy: fan_email_list | Status: building | Created: 2026-04-14*

---

## Objective
Build a direct-to-fan email list that bypasses algorithm dependency. 1,000 subscribers = one Spotify playlist placement in terms of stream impact. Goal: 5,000 subscribers in 12 months.

---

## Lead Magnet Options (pick one to launch)

### Option A — "The Descent" (Recommended)
**Offer:** Free 20-minute private DJ set recorded live on the beach in Tenerife — never released anywhere else.
**Copy:** "Before the rave. Before the stage. This is where it starts. Free, for people who find their way here."
**Why it works:** Visual (beach + sunset), unique (not on Spotify/SoundCloud), story-forward.

### Option B — Free Track Download
**Offer:** Free WAV download of "Living Water" — unreleased extended version.
**Copy:** "124 BPM. John 4. The version that never made it to Spotify."
**Why it works:** Falsifiable (specific BPM + scripture), high perceived value, drives completionists.

### Option C — Early Access
**Offer:** First listen to next release, 72 hours before anywhere else.
**Copy:** "Get it before the algorithm decides who hears it."
**Why it works:** Urgency, exclusivity, anti-algorithm positioning.

## Platform Recommendation

**ConvertKit (now Kit)** — best for creators
- Free up to 10,000 subscribers
- Automation for welcome sequences
- Integrates with music landing pages (feature.fm, Hypeddit)

**Alternative:** Mailchimp (free to 500, then paid)

## Distribution Channels for List Growth

1. **Instagram bio link** — bio.site or Linktree with email signup as top CTA
2. **TikTok/Reels CTA** — "Link in bio to hear the version that never made Spotify"
3. **Show/event QR code** — printed card or slide at gigs
4. **SoundCloud + Beatport profile** — link in description
5. **Feature.fm pre-save campaigns** — capture emails via pre-save flow
6. **Reddit/Discord community drops** — share lead magnet in relevant communities
7. **Email signature of outreach emails** — passive growth from curator conversations

## Content Calendar for List

- **Week 1 of month:** New track announcement or release
- **Week 2:** Story/behind-the-scenes (one paragraph, raw)
- **Week 3:** Curated playlist update
- **Week 4:** Ask — recommendation, feedback, share with one friend

Max 1 email/week. Quality over volume.

---

*Never use: blessed / anointed / spiritual journey / elevated consciousness / manifestation / worship music / Christian music / sober rave.*
*Always use: specific, visual, falsifiable language. One Mississippi test on every subject line.*
