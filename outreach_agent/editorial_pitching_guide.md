# Spotify Editorial Pitching Guide — Robert-Jan Mastenbroek

## What Is Editorial Pitching?
Spotify for Artists lets you pitch ONE unreleased track per release to Spotify's editorial team before it goes live. If selected, your track gets placed on an official Spotify playlist (e.g., "Techno Bunker", "Psy-Fi", "Pulse of the Earth") — which can add thousands of listeners overnight.

**This is the single highest-leverage free action available to an artist on Spotify.**

---

## Step-by-Step: How to Pitch

### 1. Log into Spotify for Artists
- Go to: https://artists.spotify.com
- Log in with your Spotify account linked to Robert-Jan Mastenbroek

### 2. Navigate to the Pitch Form
- Click **Music** in the top nav
- Select the upcoming release (must be distributed and pending — not yet live)
- Click **Pitch a Song** next to the track you want to pitch
- You can only pitch ONE track per release

### 3. Fill In Every Field — Don't Skip Any
Spotify's algorithm uses these fields to decide which editorial playlists to consider you for.

**Required fields:**

| Field | What to Write |
|-------|--------------|
| **Mood** | Select 2–3: Aggressive, Spiritual, Hypnotic, Euphoric, Intense |
| **Style** | Select 2–3: Gritty, Otherworldly, Atmospheric, Dark, Energetic |
| **Instrumentation** | Synthesizers, Electronic Drums, Bass, Percussion, Vocal Chops |
| **Story** | 500-char max — use the template below |
| **Language** | English |
| **Genre** | Electronic → Techno or Psy-Trance depending on track |
| **Sub-genre** | Psytrance, Tribal Techno, Progressive House (match to track) |
| **Culture** | Leave blank unless applicable |
| **Song Birthplace** | Tenerife, Canary Islands, Spain |
| **Release Date** | The exact Friday it drops |

## Common Mistakes That Kill Pitches

1. **Pitching a track that is already live** — you cannot pitch released music
2. **Skipping the Story field** — editors read this; it's your 15-second pitch
3. **Wrong genre/sub-genre** — "Electronic" without a sub-genre gets ignored
4. **Generic moods** — "Happy, Energetic" fits 50,000 other songs. Be specific.
5. **Pitching too late** — pitch the moment the track is distributed, at least 7 days before release

---

## What Happens After You Pitch

- Spotify editors review in 5–7 days
- You'll get a notification if selected (rare but real)
- Even if not selected for editorial, pitching improves your **algorithmic recommendations** because it tells Spotify's system your genre/mood metadata
- Pitch EVERY single release without exception — even if you never get editorial, the metadata builds up
