## 10 VIDEO CONCEPTS

### 1. "The Room Changed" — Club Crowd Reaction
**Footage:** Wide shot of dancefloor going from still to moving as track drops.
**Hook (text overlay, first 1.5s):** "Nobody was dancing. Then this happened."
**Track:** Jericho (140 BPM — the walls come down on beat 1 of bar 9)
**Caption:** The sound did that. Not me. Location: Tenerife. #techno #psytrance #livemusic
**Cut pattern:** Slow pan → drop → quick cuts synced to kick drum

### 2. "Desert vs. Club" — Split Screen
**Footage:** Ancient ruins / arid landscape / rock formations cut against dark club visuals.
**Hook:** "5,000 years old. Sounds like the future."
**Track:** Living Water (124 BPM)
**Caption:** The rhythm was already there. We just found it again. #melodictechno #ancienttechno
**Cut pattern:** Alternating 2-beat cuts, locked to 124 BPM

### 3. "The Stranger Who Stayed" — Crowd Portrait
**Footage:** Single person on dancefloor — eyes closed, clearly moved.
**Hook:** "She came alone. She stayed until the end."
**Track:** He Is The Light
**Caption:** The music found her before the words did. #holyrave #techno #djlife
**Cut pattern:** Slow zoom in (4 bars) → cut wide → slow zoom face again

### 4. "Sound Check in Paradise" — Behind the Scenes
**Footage:** RJM setting up equipment at open-air venue, sea in background, Tenerife.
**Hook:** "Sound check at the edge of the world."
**Track:** Fire In Our Hands (130 BPM)
**Caption:** Tenerife. 06:00. The only crowd so far: the Atlantic. Location: Tenerife. #djlife #producer
**Cut pattern:** Time-lapse sunrise into first bars of track

### 5. "126 BPM Tribe" — Mass Movement
**Footage:** Large crowd at outdoor rave, tribal drumming section of track peaks.
**Hook:** "126 BPM. 400 people. One heartbeat."
**Track:** Jericho or Halleluyah (140 BPM)
**Caption:** The walls always come down when the tribe shows up. Joshua 6. #psytrance #tribalrave
**Cut pattern:** Wide establishing shot → individual faces → wide again at peak

### 6. "Before and After the Drop" — Tension/Release
**Footage:** Quiet room — a few people milling around — then full dance floor after drop.
**Hook:** "The 8 seconds before everything changes."
**Track:** Halleluyah (140 BPM psytrance — use 8-bar build)
**Caption:** Some rooms just need the right key. #psytrance #livesets #drop
**Cut pattern:** Hold 8 seconds (slow motion subtle movement) → drop = fast cut montage

### 7. "Made in the Van" — Studio Origin Story
**Footage:** Campervan interior with gear, RJM producing, simple setup.
**Hook:** "Nobody wanted to book me. So I built the sound myself."
**Track:** Renamed
**Caption:** 6 years. A campervan. 30 tracks. Still going. #homeproduction #djproducer #tenerife
**Cut pattern:** Close-up hands on keys → waveform building → final mix playing

### 8. "Rave at Sunrise" — Golden Hour Set
**Footage:** Outdoor set, sun rising, crowd in silhouette.
**Hook:** "This is what the sun sounds like."
**Track:** Living Water or He Is The Light (transition when sun appears)
**Caption:** Some things you have to hear to believe. Location: Tenerife. #sunrise #techno #holyrave
**Cut pattern:** Time-lapse sunrise locked to tempo of track

### 9. "The Set They Didn't Book" — Underdog Arc
**Footage:** RJM playing to a small group / open space / free show.
**Hook:** "They said the crowd was too small. The crowd disagreed."
**Track:** Fire In Our Hands
**Caption:** Every big room started with a small one. #djlife #underground #techno
**Cut pattern:** Wide shot → zoom crowd size → reaction shots → big finish

### 10. "One Track. Two Worlds." — Sacred vs. Secular Edit
**Footage:** Split: church-style architecture exterior + dark rave interior.
**Hook:** "Built in the same key. Different rooms."
**Track:** Renamed (Isaiah 62 anchor — new name = new identity)
**Caption:** "You will be called by a new name." Isaiah 62. The sound knows. #holyrave #melodictechno
**Cut pattern:** Parallel cuts on beat — sacred/secular alternating at 2-bar intervals
