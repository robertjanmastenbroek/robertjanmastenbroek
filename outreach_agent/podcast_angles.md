# Podcast Pitch Angles — Robert-Jan Mastenbroek
*Last updated: 2026-04-08*

---

## ANGLE 1 — The Entrepreneur Collapse Story
**Headline:** "From €6M to Zero: What Losing Everything Taught Me About Sound"

**Pitch:**
Robert-Jan built a €6M platform in the Netherlands, lost it all at 30, and rebuilt his life in Tenerife through music. He now makes Bible-inspired techno and psytrance — weekly releases, 290K Instagram followers, growing Spotify presence. This is a show about losing it all and finding something better on the other side.

**Best for:** Entrepreneurship pods, success/failure pods, mindset shows, expat lifestyle shows

**Key talking points:**
- The moment the business collapsed and what he did next
- Why Tenerife? The specific decision to start over on an island
- How music became both therapy and business
- What he'd tell his 30-year-old self
- The relationship between failure and creativity

## ANGLE 2 — Faith Meets the Dancefloor
**Headline:** "Bible-Inspired Techno: Making Sacred Music for People Who Don't Go to Church"

**Pitch:**
Robert-Jan Mastenbroek makes psytrance and tribal techno inspired by the Bible — not worship music, not gospel, but 140 BPM electronic music with biblical themes woven into the sound and titles. He's reaching people with faith through a genre they'd never expect it in.

- Making music for the person who left the church but still believes
- How tracks like "Jericho," "Halleluyah," and "Living Water" were created
- The theology of sound — music as prayer, the dancefloor as sanctuary
- Why the underground electronic scene is spiritually hungry

## ANGLE 3 — The Expat Reinvention Story
**Headline:** "Left the Netherlands, Moved to Tenerife, Built a Music Career From Nothing"

**Key talking points:**
- The decision to leave the Netherlands
- What Tenerife offers that the Netherlands couldn't
- Building a creative life in a place where you know no one
- The practical realities of being an artist on an island
- What he misses and what he'd never go back to

## ANGLE 4 — The Weekly Release Discipline
**Headline:** "Releasing Music Every Friday: What Shipping Weekly Taught Me About Creativity and Growth"

**Pitch:**
Robert-Jan releases a new track every single Friday — no exceptions. This isn't about quantity over quality. It's a deliberate system for staying creative, building an audience, and treating art like a practice rather than a performance. He'll share exactly how he does it.

**Best for:** Creative entrepreneurship pods, productivity shows, artist business shows, creator economy shows

**Key talking points:**
- Why weekly releases over monthly albums
- How he maintains quality at high volume
- The creative process: from idea to released track in 7 days
- What weekly shipping does for your Spotify algorithm
- The mindset shift from "perfectionist" to "practitioner"
