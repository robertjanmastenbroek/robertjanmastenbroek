## Submission Rules (Apply to All)

1. **Format:** Private SoundCloud or WeTransfer WAV link — never public until placed
2. **Max tracks per submission:** 3 (Stil Vor Talent, Anjunadeep enforce this)
3. **Best send day:** Tuesday or Thursday morning CET
4. **Bio angle:** Lead with music identity ("126 BPM tribal pulse, recorded Tenerife") — scripture anchor subtle in artist story
5. **Subject line:** Under 6 words, no artist name — lead with genre/BPM/mood
6. **Follow up:** Once, after 7 days — if no response, move on

## Pay-to-Play Warning

Spotify prohibits pay-for-placement playlists. Any curator charging for guaranteed placement violates ToS — their playlists get removed. Stick to free/merit-based above. The one exception: **Groover** ($2/submission guaranteed feedback) is legitimate because it pays for *feedback*, not placement.
