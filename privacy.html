Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

This website is operated by Robert-Jan Mastenbroek, an independent music artist based in Tenerife, Spain. Contact: motomotosings@gmail.com

What data we collect

We do not collect personal data through this website. We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site. This collects anonymised data including pages visited, time on site, and general location (country level). No personally identifiable information is stored.

If you contact us via email, we store only the information you provide in order to respond to your message.

Social media integrations

This site links to third-party platforms including TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and Spotify. When you follow links to these platforms, their own privacy policies apply. We use the TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube APIs to publish content (music videos and short clips) to our own accounts on those platforms. We do not collect or store data about third-party users through these APIs.

Cookies

We use cookies only through Google Analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across other websites.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law.

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at motomotosings@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
