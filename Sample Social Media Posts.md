### Post 2: Practical Faith
**Caption:**
5 simple ways to share your faith this week:

1. Actually listen when someone shares a struggle — then ask if you can pray for them
2. Share what you're learning in your Bible reading (not preaching, just sharing)
3. Invite someone to church, a small group, or even just coffee
4. Tell someone specifically what you're grateful to God for
5. When things go well, give credit to God out loud

Evangelism isn't always a big moment. Often it's small, consistent, genuine.

Which one will you try this week?

#ShareYourFaith #Evangelism #ChristianLiving #FaithInAction #Gospel

## INSTAGRAM REELS / TIKTOK SCRIPTS

### Reel 1: Quick Encouragement (30-60 seconds)
**Hook (text on screen):** "If you're feeling overwhelmed today, watch this"

**Script:**
"Hey, if you're feeling overwhelmed, anxious, or like everything is too much right now — I just want to remind you of something.

In Matthew 11:28, Jesus said: 'Come to me, all who labor and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest.'

He didn't say 'figure it out first.' He didn't say 'clean yourself up.' He said COME.

Right now. As you are. With all of it.

You don't have to carry this alone."

**End text:** "He's got you. 🙏"

---

### Reel 2: Apologetics Hook (45-60 seconds)
**Hook (text on screen):** "Why would a good God allow suffering?"

**Script:**
"This is probably the #1 question people ask about Christianity. And honestly? It's a fair question.

Here's the short answer: God didn't create suffering. We did. Humanity chose rebellion against God, and suffering entered the world through that choice.

But here's what makes Christianity different: God didn't just watch from a distance. He entered into our suffering. Jesus experienced poverty, rejection, betrayal, torture, and death.

He gets it. And he promises that one day, every tear will be wiped away.

I have a longer video explaining this — link in bio."

**End CTA:** Follow for more

---

### Reel 3: Day in the Life with Faith (60 seconds)
**Hook (text on screen):** "How I start my morning with God"

**Visual:** Show yourself doing each thing

**Script/Voiceover:**
"Here's my simple morning routine that keeps me grounded:

First, I don't touch my phone for the first 10 minutes. Instead, I just thank God for the new day.

Then I read just one chapter of the Bible. Not a whole book — just one chapter. Today it was Psalm 23.

I write down one thing that stood out to me.

Then I pray. Nothing fancy — just talking to God like He's right there. Because He is.

It takes about 20 minutes and it completely changes my day.

What does your morning routine look like?"

## TIPS FOR USING THESE TEMPLATES

1. **Make them yours:** Edit the language to match your voice
2. **Add your story:** Personal touches make content connect
3. **Use relevant hashtags:** Research what's working in your niche
4. **Engage:** Respond to every comment, especially in the first hour
5. **Be consistent:** Better to post less often than to burn out
6. **Test and learn:** See what resonates with YOUR audience

Remember: The goal isn't viral content. It's faithful presence.
