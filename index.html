What is Selah?

In the Psalms, Selah is a pause — a moment to stop, reflect, and let the music land. Not silence. A breath between movements. The ancient instruction to be still.

SELAH is RJM's first dedicated live event series. An underground rave built on sacred architecture — tribal rhythm, deep melodic pressure, and the presence that follows both.

The walls came down at Jericho. The music played first.
